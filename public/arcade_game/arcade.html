How to Play Space Shooter

  • Use the left and right arrow keys to move your spaceship (🌍).
  • Press the spacebar to shoot bullets and destroy pollutants.
  • Avoid letting pollutants reach the bottom of the screen.
  • Each destroyed pollutant increases your score by 1.
  • You start with 5 lives. Lose a life when a pollutant reaches the bottom.
  • The game ends when you lose all lives or reach a score of 20.
