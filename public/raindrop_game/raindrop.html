How to Play Cloud Catcher

  • Move the cloud left and right to catch falling water drops.
  • Use left and right arrow keys on desktop, or touch the left/right side of the screen on mobile.
  • Each caught drop increases your score by 1.
  • You start with 6 lives. Lose a life when a drop reaches the bottom.
  • The game ends when you lose all lives or reach a score of 10.
