Green City Builder

Welcome to Green City Builder! In this game, you can design a sustainable city by selecting various options that either positively or negatively impact the environment. Each choice affects your score and budget. Your goal is to maximize your score while managing your coins wisely. Click on the grid to place your chosen options and see how sustainable your city can be!
